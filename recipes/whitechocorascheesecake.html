White Chocolate Raspberry Cheesecake - Allrecipes

Description

Looking for a dessert to wow a crowd? Itching to make something for a special occasion? Here's something that is made to amaze your guests: white chocolate raspberry cheesecake! In no time at all, you'll have a delectable after-dinner treat that will look and taste just as good as a restaurant ordered one. Don't believe me? Try it yourself! Want to add some more pizzazz? Sprinkle and dust with some chocolate curls (for that extranness, of course.)

Ingredients

Crust

  • 1 cup chocolate cookie crumbs
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar

Sauce

  • 1 (10 ounce) package frozen raspberries
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch

Cheesecake

  • 2 cups white chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup half-and-half cream
  • 3 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2
    • cup white sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Make crust: Mix together cookie crumbs, melted butter, and sugar in a medium bowl. Press mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan.
  2. Make sauce: Combine raspberries, water, sugar, and cornstarch in a saucepan. Bring to a boil and continue boiling until sauce is thick, about 5 minutes. Strain through a mesh strainer to remove seeds. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
  3. Make cheesecake: Melt white chocolate chips with half-and-half in a metal bowl over a pan of simmering water; stir occasionally until smooth.
  4. Mix together cream cheese and sugar in a large bowl until smooth. Beat in eggs one at a time. Blend in vanilla and melted white chocolate mixture. Pour 1/2 of the batter over crust.
  5. Spoon 3 tablespoons raspberry sauce over batter. Pour remaining cheesecake batter on top. Spoon 3 tablespoons raspberry sauce over batter. Swirl batter with the tip of a knife to create a marbled effect.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until filling is set, 55 to 60 minutes. Cool cheesecake at room temperature, then cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate until thoroughly chilled, 8 hours to overnight.
  7. Remove cheesecake from the pan and serve with remaining raspberry sauce.
